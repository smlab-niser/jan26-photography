NISER Sports Day 2025 - Photography

  • Binilkrishna V S (Int. MSc)

Important Dates

  • Start Date: 10th Jan, 2025
  • Submission Deadline: 24th Jan, 2025
  • Announcement of Results: 26th Jan, 2025

Submission

Submission is through Google Form (Will be made live on 24th Jan, 2025)

Tasks

Main Tasks (7 photos x 10 points)

  • Motion Capture: Take a perfectly timed photo of a moving ball during a game.
  • Sports Emotions: Capture raw emotions, celebrations, frustration.
  • Dynamic Action Shot: Photograph an athlete mid-action, like jumping, running, or scoring.
  • Team Spirit: Showcase teamwork or a group huddle in an engaging composition.
  • Unique Perspective: Shoot from an unusual angle to show the game in a new way.
  • Pre-Game Rituals: Document warm-ups, preparations, or rituals before the game begins.
  • Playground Symmetry: Capture symmetrical lines or patterns on the sports field or court.

Bonus Tasks (6 photos x 5 points)

  • Selfie with a Player: Take a selfie with any athlete during the event.
  • Snack Time: Capture someone enjoying snacks or drinks during the break.
  • Best Pose: Ask any athlete to strike a quirky or creative pose for your photo.
  • Shadow Play: Take a photo of a cool shadow on the field or court.
  • Animal Cameo: Snap a photo of any animal that appears at the venue.
  • Fan Frenzy: Capture the energy of the audience with their expressions or movements.
Note: Any photo submitted may be freely used by the organizing committee for promotional or other purposes.

Rules

  1. Main tasks will be critically judged on creativity, relevance, and quality.
  2. Bonus tasks provide extra points to boost scores.
  3. Points will be awarded for task completion and photo quality.
  4. Each task will have different points associated with it, and the team with the maximum points wins.
  5. There is no specified order for completing the tasks.
  6. The captures should be taken only during the event period.
  7. A single device should be used to take all the captures (you might want to keep the device charged).
  8. Both mobile devices and cameras are allowed.
  9. Photos must be in JPG/JPEG format with intact EXIF data. Avoid platforms that corrupt EXIF data.
  10. Tasks can be completed in any order, but only one entry per task is allowed.
  11. Basic editing like sharpening and color correction is allowed, but no heavy alterations.
  12. All photos must be original captures by the team.
  13. Submissions will be managed online via the representative’s contact details.
  14. By submitting, participants grant Sapsara the right to use photos for social media or other purposes.
  15. Organizers' decisions are final in case of disputes.

Results
